# Pitch Notes

## Problem

Municipal complaint systems are often fragmented, text-heavy, and slow to route. Citizens do not know which department owns an issue, and staff spend time triaging repetitive reports.

## Solution

CivicEye AI gives citizens a simple multimodal complaint flow and gives officials an AI-assisted operations dashboard.

## Differentiators

- Voice-first and multilingual-ready intake.
- AI routing to departments.
- Duplicate and urgency signals.
- Public tracking ID for trust and transparency.
- Lightweight service architecture that can be deployed city by city.
