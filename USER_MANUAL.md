# CivicEye AI India User Manual

## Introduction
CivicEye AI India is a multilingual, AI-powered civic grievance assistance platform designed to help citizens report public issues quickly and clearly. It accepts complaints through text, voice, image, and location inputs, then converts them into structured grievance tickets for easier processing by government departments.

This manual explains how citizens can submit and track complaints, and how administrators can manage complaints and monitor platform activity.

## Platform Purpose
The platform is built to simplify civic complaint reporting and improve communication between citizens and government departments.

Key capabilities include:
- Multilingual complaint submission.
- AI-assisted complaint structuring.
- Evidence upload through images.
- Location-based issue reporting.
- Complaint tracking for citizens.
- Complaint management and analytics for administrators.

# Citizen Portal User Guide

## Step 1: Access the Portal

Open the CivicEye AI India Portal using your web browser.

* No registration or login is required.
* You can begin submitting a complaint immediately.

---

## Step 2: Submit a Complaint

From the home dashboard, select **Report an Issue**.

You may provide complaint details using one or more of the following methods:

### Text Submission

Enter your complaint in any supported language.

**Example:**

> Street lights near the bus stand have not been functioning for several days.

### Voice Submission

* Click the **Microphone** icon.
* Speak your complaint clearly.
* The system automatically converts your speech into text.

### Image Upload

Upload supporting photographs related to the issue, such as:

* Potholes or damaged roads
* Overflowing garbage bins
* Water leakage problems
* Damaged public infrastructure

### Location Information

You can:

* Allow automatic location detection, or
* Manually enter or select the complaint location.

---

## Step 3: AI-Powered Processing

Once the complaint is entered, the platform automatically:

* Converts voice recordings into text.
* Identifies the language of the complaint.
* Generates a brief complaint summary.
* Classifies the issue category.
* Recommends the appropriate government department.
* Assigns a priority level based on urgency.

---

## Step 4: Verify and Submit

Review the generated complaint details before submission:

* Complaint summary
* Issue category
* Recommended department
* Priority level

If the information is accurate, click **Submit Complaint**.

---

## Step 5: Monitor Complaint Progress

Go to **My Complaints** to track submitted issues.

The portal displays:

* Complaint/Ticket ID
* Current complaint status
* Assigned department
* Date of submission
* Resolution and progress updates

You can check the status of your complaint at any time through the portal.

## Admin Dashboard

### Dashboard Overview
The administrator dashboard allows authorized users to monitor and manage all complaints submitted through the platform.

Administrators can:
- View all complaints.
- Filter complaints by category.
- Filter complaints by state.
- Filter complaints by department.
- View complaint analytics.

### Complaint Management
Each complaint record may contain:
- Citizen details.
- Complaint summary.
- Category.
- Priority level.
- Location.
- Uploaded evidence.

Administrators can perform the following actions:
- Update complaint status.
- Assign or reassign departments.
- Add internal remarks.

### Analytics Section
The dashboard may display visual analytics such as:
- Complaint trends.
- Department-wise distribution.
- State-wise complaints.
- High-priority issues.
- Resolution statistics.

## Troubleshooting

### Voice Input Not Working
Try the following:
- Check whether microphone permissions are enabled.
- Refresh the browser and try again.
- Ensure the microphone is connected and functioning properly.

### Image Upload Failed
Try the following:
- Verify that the file size is within the allowed limit.
- Check your internet connection.
- Retry the upload using a supported image format.

### Location Unavailable
Try the following:
- Enable GPS or browser location permissions.
- Refresh the page.
- Enter the location manually if automatic detection fails.
