# CivicEye AI India Code of Conduct

## Purpose
CivicEye AI India is committed to building a respectful, inclusive, and safe environment for all participants. This Code of Conduct sets expectations for behaviour within the project community and establishes a clear process for reporting and addressing misconduct.

This document applies to all members, contributors, maintainers, collaborators, and participants involved in CivicEye AI India project spaces, discussions, repositories, and related communications.

## Our Pledge
Members, contributors, and maintainers of CivicEye AI India pledge to make participation in the project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

The project is committed to fostering an open and professional community in which all individuals are treated with dignity, fairness, and respect.

## Expected Standards
Positive behaviour includes:

- Using welcoming, inclusive, and respectful language.
- Being considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Demonstrating empathy toward other community members.
- Supporting collaboration in a professional and solution-oriented manner.

## Unacceptable Behaviour
The following behaviours are considered unacceptable:

- Sexualised language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Trolling, insults, derogatory comments, or personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing or threatening to publish another person's private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate, intimidating, abusive, or harmful in a professional community setting.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies in all CivicEye AI India community spaces, including but not limited to:

- Source code repositories.
- Issue trackers.
- Merge requests and pull requests.
- Project documentation.
- Community discussions.
- Official communication channels associated with the project.

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public or professional settings.

## Reporting and Enforcement
Violations of this Code of Conduct may be reported confidentially to:

**Email:** [conduct@civiceye.example.com](mailto:conduct@civiceye.example.com)

All reports will be reviewed promptly, fairly, and confidentially. Every reasonable effort will be made to protect the privacy and safety of the reporting party.

Project maintainers are responsible for investigating reported incidents and determining an appropriate response based on the severity, frequency, and impact of the behaviour.

## Consequences
The following enforcement guidelines may be applied depending on the nature of the violation:

| Violation Level | Consequence |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Minor / First Instance | Written warning |
| Repeated / Moderate | Temporary ban |
| Severe | Permanent ban |

Corrective actions may also include removal of content, restricted participation, or additional moderation measures where necessary.

## Commitment to Fair Process
Enforcement decisions will be made with professionalism, consistency, and proportionality. The goal of enforcement is to protect the community, uphold project standards, and encourage respectful participation.

Retaliation against any person who reports misconduct in good faith will not be tolerated.
